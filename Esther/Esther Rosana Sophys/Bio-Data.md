### Full Name
- Esther Rosana Sophys
### Meaning
- ***Esther**:*   From the Biblical Queen Esther. The name Esther is of Persian origin and is thought to be derived from the Old Persian word 'Stara', meaning 'star'. It's also been linked to the Babylonian goddess Isthar, associated with love, beauty, and the planet Venus, further connecting it to celestial imagery.  
- **Rosana:*** Spanish or Portuguse variant of the name 'Roxane', descended from the Persian 'Rauxshna'. Carries meanings of 'Dawn', 'Sunrise', 'Star of Magnificence'.
- **Sophys:*** Traditional Persian last name that references the location of the person's birth i.e. Sophia Island.
