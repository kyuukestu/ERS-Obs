A vagabond from Orre with a knack for tinkering and a penchant for amassing wealth and power. She's salvaged a Snag Machine but hasn't been able to restore it to operational capacity.

A skilled tracker, battler and poacher, she's been selling Pokemon on the black market to enrich herself. She's follow-up on the legacy of Hunter J, the most infamous Pokemon Poacher of all time, and was able to salvage tools and equipment from the wreckage of J's ship.

Upgrades:
- Snag Machine
- Mirage Pokemon
- Shadow Mirages
